About Me

My name is Manish Tamta. I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Centre for High Energy Physics (CHEP) at IISc Bangalore. I completed M.S. in physics at IISc and B.Sc. (H) Physics at St. Stephen's College, University of Delhi. My research interests include Astroparticle physics and Cosmology. I am engaged in a research project related to dark matter, previously I did some work in numerical simulations. Apart from physics, I enjoy traveling and photography.
